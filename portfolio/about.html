About Me

Chloe King, internet alias Shakoba, is a prominent fan artist who started her passion at a young age. She started off with traditional drawing as a child, until she gained inspiration from watching speedpaint videos on YouTube, which motivated her to began digital art.

Her earliest moments of promoting her art online was through DeviantArt. She was a fan of Thomas and Friends at the time, and made fan art which engaged a fair share of followers. After being active in the DeviantArt TTTE community, she's moved on to other special interests and shared her work to Instagram, Reddit and YouTube.

Although she is skilled at fan creations, she hopes to use her skills for creative works in the animation industry and the gaming community.
