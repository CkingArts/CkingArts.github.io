About Me

Chloe King, internet alias Shakoba (she/her), is a prominent fan artist who started her passion at a young age. She started off with traditional drawing as a child. She began doing her primary medium of art, digital art, since she was 10 yearsold.

Her earliest moments of promoting her art online was through DeviantArt. Her fan art started to gain popularity as she did creative works for niche fandoms.

Although she is skilled at fan creations, Shakoba is studying under the DMA program at San Jose State University. Once she gets her BFA, she plans on doing computer art for the animation and gaming industry.
